What goes into the model

  • Existing rankings (Keyword Opps): the value of moving already-ranked terms into higher positions, using the CTR lift between current and optimized rank.
  • New targets (New Targets): the value of earning first-time rankings for currently unranked, in-market keywords.
  • Seasonality: monthly organic sessions from the trailing 12 months shape the ramp so each month reflects real demand patterns.
  • Traffic → revenue: projected sessions × 6.0% CVR × $500 lead value.
