What goes into the model

  • Current performance: the trailing-12-month average sessions, leads, and revenue — the no-change baseline.
  • Post-optimization keyword value: the added sessions, leads, and revenue from the keyword targets — both moving already-ranked terms up and earning new rankings — summed from the keyword table.
  • Ramp & seasonality: optimization value phases in linearly from 1% in month 1 to 100% in the final month, then each month is scaled by its share of annual organic traffic.

New keyword targets — paste & auto-calculate

Paste keywords from the New Targets or Keyword Opps tabs (Keyword, Current Position, Search Volume, KD). Estimated traffic is the optimized-traffic formula: Search Volume × the Position Key matched to each keyword’s current position (4–10, 11–20, or 21–30) and KD tier. A position of 0 is treated as a brand-new target and uses the 11–20 key. Transactions and revenue then apply your CVR and lead value. When this table has rows, its totals populate the Post-Optimization · Ranked Keywords group above; that group stays at zero until you add keyword targets.
